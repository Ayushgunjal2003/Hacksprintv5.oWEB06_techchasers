The CoWIN vaccine certificate is a digital document issued by the Government of India through the CoWIN portal.

You can download your CoWIN vaccine certificate through the CoWIN portal or the Aarogya Setu app. Simply log in with your registered mobile number and download the certificate from the "Certificate" section. You can choose to download a PDF or save it to your digital wallet for easy access.
